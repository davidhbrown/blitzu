Blitzu Cyclops 120H Simulator

A state machine example based on one of my older bicycle headlights. Here are links to the product page [via web.archive.org] and you to an instruction video on YouTube (unfortunately now marked private and not viewable). Use the red button to operate the headlight. The headlight responds differently to quick and to 2-second presses.

 

Seizure Warning:

For about 3 percent of people with epilepsy, exposure to flashing lights at certain intensities or to certain visual patterns can trigger seizures. This program produces high-constrast flashes at 2Hz and 8Hz, within the 5Hz-30Hz range most likely to trigger seizures.

(Source: Epilepsy Foundation)
